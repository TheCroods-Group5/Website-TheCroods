ONLINE GAMING ADDICTION

This website tackles the topic Online Gaming Addiction: How it affects the overall health of a teenage student, also focusing on the team's events in streaming.

Get Started

Introduction

Online games have been and will continue to be one of the most popular hobbies for teens due to how accessible they are at current times. It provides stimulation that is great for entertainment and satisfaction to people of all ages. Moreover, they are designed to be addictive using state-of-the-art behavioral psychology to keep you hooked. But the same with other sports, it also has its own disadvantages. With this, as students and individuals who value health, digging deeper into the causes and effects of online gaming addiction would teach a variety of people to take appropriate action.

About Us

Definition

Online gaming addiction is the compulsive or uncontrolled use of video games, in a way that causes problems in other areas of a person's life.

Causes

There are many causes of online gaming addiction which may include escapism, parental neglection, social acceptance, or mental health problems.

Effects

Online gaming addiction can greatly put a person's health at risk, may that be physically, mentally, or socially.

Preventions

Preventions may include having a break, parent supervision, trying different hobbies, or bonding with others.

What is streaming?

Streaming refers to watching videos or listening to audio content without having the need to download it first.



What are the types and platforms for streaming?

You may watch or listen to many different types of streaming such as movies, music videos, video games, TV shows, and others on streaming platforms like Netflix, Spotify, YouTube, Twitch, Facebook, etc. Note that it is important that you are able to upload appropriate content to your designated streaming platform.

Survey Release

Our group has conducted a survey to dig deeper into the thoughts and opinions of viewers on 3 certain contents; Educational Videos, Community Based Videos, and Video Podcasts.


Beliefs and Opinions on the Topic

  • 48 out of the 50 respondents have knowledge as to what/how online games work. 2 respondents, however, do not know what/how online games work.
  • Call Of Duty is the most popular video game among all 50 students, accumulating 42/50 votes. Fortnite, on the contrary, has the least votes having a total of 26/50.
  • 42 students think that online gaming addiction is not okay. 8 students, on the other hand, think that online gaming addiction is okay.
  • 30 respondents have experienced being bullied through online gaming. 20 respondents have not experienced being bullied through online gaming.

    • Attitudes on the Topic

    • 45 students are aware of how online gaming addiction affects their health. 2 students are not, while 3 students do not care.
    • 25 respondents always play online games. The other 25 respondents, however, do not always play online games.
    • 49 students believe online gaming is fun. 1 student, on the other hand, does not believe online gaming is fun.

What do we need to learn?

Online Gaming may appear to be good for building up character development, but the lack of self-limitation of teenage students to this is closely linked to online gaming addiction, greatly affecting our well-being, whether socially, mentally, physically, or emotionally. Thus, the viewers need to:

  • Know how online gaming addiction affects the overall health of a player.
  • Manage their time when playing online games to avoid addiction.
  • Analyze what online games are appropriate for their age.
  • Learn ways how to overcome online gaming addiction.

A stream should equip the following characteristics for good performance.

  • Know the trends.

    • When starting a stream or uploading content, it's important to keep up with the trends. This makes your content more interesting and engaging and it helps you create content that your audience wants as well, allowing you to increase the number of your viewers.

  • Be clear.

    • Your content should be clear to make your audiences' viewing experience understandable.

  • Engage.

    • Engaging with your audience keeps things enjoyable and gets people to stick around. Low chance of boredom and a high chance of fun!

  • Be energetic.

    • It's best to maintain a fun stream with energetic energy as this is one of the most important keys to having a successful stream.

  • Don't forget to be professional!

    • Executing professionalism in appearance, way of talking, gestures, and the overall process of streaming allow your content to be appropriate for a variety of individuals.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Live Streaming?

    Live Streaming is type of streaming that refers to a media content that allows us to watch and/or listen in real time. During live streaming, pausing the video or audio content may not be done. You cannot go back to where you left it.

  • What are some common public live streaming applications?

    Some of the public live streaming applications include Facebook Live, Twitter, Instagram Live Stories, V Live, Twitch TV, Tik Tok, and YouTube Live.

  • What is the importance of learning about online gaming addiction?

    Learning about this topic is very important as the gaming industry has greatly evolved over the years. Individuals have become more vulnerable to addiction toward online gaming, especially teenage students like us as we use this as a way for escapism.

  • Why did we choose online gaming addiction as our topic?

    As online games have become more popular, spreading awareness about this topic will allow individuals to keep in mind the dangers of spending too much time on online games to the point that it affects their overall health and lifestyle.
