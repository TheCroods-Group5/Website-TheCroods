Quarter 2: Live Stream Layout Designs

As our topic is online gaming addiction, we focused on the words "Online Gaming" and thought of colors that pop up to mind. With this, according to our experience, gaming streamers tend to use the colors in the galaxy; pink, purple, blue, and white. Thus, to capture the attention of the audience and to address the needs of our viewers, the group has decided that giving them something interesting may start with giving them attention-seeking designs and colors.

Quarter 3: Live Stream Presentation

In line with the team's streaming outline, the presentation covers the causes, effects, testimonials concerning the causes and effects, as well as some preventions to online gaming addiction. All information gathered are from research and cited.

Quarter 3: Live Stream Data Analytics

The presentation shows the collected data analytics from our Facebook live stream as well as the survey analytics from the released live stream feedback form. In addition, after analyzing the figures and information gathered, the team has known its strengths, opportunities for improvement, and achieved goals.
